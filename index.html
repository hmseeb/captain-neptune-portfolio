// ABOUT THE CAPTAIN

MASTER OF
THE DEEP CRAFT

Born in the storm-swept Azores, raised by tide and current, Captain Erasmus Neptune discovered the ancient art of underwater basket weaving at the age of seven, guided by a wise old octopus named Gerald.

After three decades beneath the waves — surviving kraken encounters, rogue currents, and the occasional aggressive crab — the Captain has woven over 4,200 baskets and trained more than 800 students, including 47 certified mermaids.

His technique, known as the "Neptune Spiral Knot", is recognized internationally as the gold standard of aquatic basket construction.
